Privacy Policy

BizzDeck (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the BizzDeck website and mobile applications on Android and iOS (collectively, the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

At BizzDeck, our mission is to simplify restaurant management and empower business owners with clear, data-driven insights. We help restaurants make sense of complex reports, reconcile online sales, and turn day-to-day challenges into measurable growth opportunities.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following personally identifiable information:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address (City, State/Province, ZIP/Postal Code)

Business & Document Information

Restaurant owners may upload Statement of Account (SoA) documents from platforms such as Swiggy and Zomato. These documents are used only to analyze reports, reconcile online sales, and generate insights.

Usage Data & Analytics

We use Google Analytics to monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve the performance and usability of our Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service
  • Generate insights and reports
  • Manage user accounts
  • Process consultation bookings
  • Communicate with users
  • Improve our website and mobile applications
  • Ensure platform security

3. Email Communications

We may contact users via email for account updates, service notifications, consultation confirmations, and support. Gmail is used as our email service provider.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience and analyze usage trends. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Advertisements

BizzDeck does not display advertisements and does not use user data for advertising or ad targeting.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal and business data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal and uploaded data only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

8. Third-Party Services

Our Service may use third-party tools such as Google Analytics and Gmail. These services have their own privacy policies governing their data practices.

9. Children’s Privacy

BizzDeck does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
